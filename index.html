About the Project

Eco-Insights provides real-time monitoring of air and water pollution levels. By analyzing environmental data with AI, Eco-Insights offers valuable insights and predictions to protect our planet.

Features

Real-Time Data Collection

Gathers and processes data from environmental sensors to provide up-to-the-minute pollution levels.

AI-Powered Analytics

Uses machine learning to predict pollution trends and detect anomalies, helping prevent crises.

Interactive Dashboard

Visualizes data on an intuitive, easy-to-read dashboard with maps and trend graphs.
